Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the online reminder feature work?
A: The online reminder feature allows you to set reminders for upcoming bill due dates. You can specify the frequency and time of the reminder, and you will receive notifications via email to ensure you never miss a payment..
Q: How do I sign up for an account?
A: To sign up for an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the required information in the registration form.
Q: Can I set multiple reminders?
A: Yes, you can set multiple reminders. Simply navigate to the "Reminders" page after logging in and click on the "Add Reminder" button to create a new reminder.
Q: Can I schedule multiple reminders for different bills?
A: Yes, you can schedule multiple reminders for different bills. The website provides a flexible interface that allows you to manage and organize your bills effectively.
Q: How do I edit or delete a reminder?
A: On the "Reminders" page, you will see a list of your existing reminders. To edit or delete a reminder, click on the corresponding buttons next to each reminder entry.
Q: Can I add custom reminders for non-recurring expenses?
A: Yes, you can add custom reminders for any type of expense, whether it's a bill or a one-time payment. The website allows you to create personalized reminders to keep track of important dates and events.
Q: Is my personal and financial information secure on the website?
A: Absolutely. We prioritize the security and privacy of your personal and financial information.
Q: Is there a mobile app available for the website?
A: We are still working on it. When the work was done we will notify you.
